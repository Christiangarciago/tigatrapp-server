About Mosquitto Alert

This project has been made possible thanks to the growing support of a number of public and private entities, as well as many professionals.

The project is coordinated by the ICREA Movement Ecology Laboratory at CEAB-CSIC and CREAF.

The project has been driven by “la Caixa” Banking Foundation.

The project is cofounded by the Independent Public Health Body of Girona Provincial Council (Dipsalut), the Ministry of Economy and Competitiveness, the Spanish Foundation for Science and Technology (FECYT) and the private company lokímica.

Many of the photorgaphs in the app Mosquito Alert have been donated by R. Eritja.
