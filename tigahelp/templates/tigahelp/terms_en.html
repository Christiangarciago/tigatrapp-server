User Agreement

Mosquito Alert is a citizen science project being implemented by the ICREA-Movement Ecology Laboratory at the Centre for Advanced Studies of Blanes (CEAB-CSIC) in cooperation with the Spanish National Research Council and the University of Murcia.

The project relies on members of the public—citizen scientists—to collect data on the spread of tiger mosquitoes and yellow fever mosquitoes. You can participate by sharing data through any of the Mosquito Alert applications for mobile devices.
