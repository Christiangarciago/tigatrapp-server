Charts directory

  • Speed Meter - shows number of reports that entered the system last week, as well as average number of reports per day (last 7 days). This graph self updates every 10 seconds.
  • Pie chart adults - Shows number of adult observations geographically, with pie charts showing different categories of adult observations. The size of the pies indicates the number of observations.
  • Pie chart sites - Same as above, with breeding sites
  • Province sampling comparison, adults - Shows a cloropleth map with number of observations determining color intensity. When click in a province, it shows a mini-graph with the sampling distribution in the province by year. With Shift+click, multiple provinces can be selected and compared.
  • Province sampling comparison, sites - Same as above, with breeding sites
  • Adult and breeding site sampling, monthly - Side by side comparison of number of reports per month, with selectable categories
